Librevault 0.1.15

May 30, 2016

We have a first public release, 0.1.15!

I am very glad to present you a first public Librevault release, version 0.1.15!
This is an early development version, still having *many** bugs and missing features, but working.

Disclaimer: It is a very early alpha version, don't use it in the production environment. The performance may be very low, memory/CPU footprint may be high and the bugs may cause the loss of data.
If you see any bugs, please file an issue to our issues page or to our community forum.
